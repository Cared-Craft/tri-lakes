# GSBOR Plus - Greater Springfield Board of Realtors

## GSBOR+ Partner Program

### Connecting REALTORS® with Small Business Owners

The Greater Springfield Board of REALTORS® Plus Program was designed to create and foster ongoing relationships with GSBOR members (REALTORS® and local affiliates) and small business owners by offering a discount or incentive to GSBOR members to shop with and refer business to local small business owners.

Local small business owners are the heartbeat of the Ozarks. They stimulate the economy, create jobs, foster innovation, and make our community an all-around more desirable place to live. As REALTORS® who serve as ambassadors and champions of our community, we love small business owners, and we believe we can help.

#### GSBOR Member Advantages

1. Reputation. The GSBOR+ Program helps GSBOR members establish and uphold
a reputation for championing local businesses as they do business with and
promote GSBOR+ Partners.

2. Relationships. The GSBOR+ Program helps GSBOR members develop ongoing
relationships with local small business owners and their employees through
frequent interactions. Remember, your network builds your net worth!

3. Discounts. The GSBOR+ Program gives members an incentive to not only shop
locally, but to refer friends, family, colleagues, and past clients to each Partner.

To redeem your discount on eligible purchases, access your GSBOR+ digital card using the button below. Present to the local participating business on your mobile device. Discounts apply to the GSBOR member only.
