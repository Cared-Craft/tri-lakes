# Forms - Greater Springfield Board of Realtors

## Current and New Member Forms

Submit completed form to support@gsbor.com or to contact us page.

Answers to your MLS and listing questions, troubleshooting, and guidebooks.

## Membership Changes or Deactivations

Please email a staff member at support@gsbor.com for further help if you are changing brokers or canceling your membership.

*Note: If canceling membership, your license must be held by a referral/holding company, a broker who does not hold Board/MLS membership, or back to the MREC.
