# Realtor Safety Information - Greater Springfield Board of Realtors

## FOREWARN

GSBOR is excited to partner with FOREWARN to bring you cutting-edge technology designed to improve REALTOR® safety and security. All primary and secondary GSBOR REALTOR® members are eligible for Premium app features at no additional cost.

FOREWARN brings knowledge to the real estate industry through innovative solutions to ensure safer engagements and smarter interactions. Leveraging powerful analytics and a massive data repository, FOREWARN solutions enable real estate professionals to gain real-time knowledge, for purposes such as verifying identity, searching for criminal histories, and validating information provided by potential clients such as financial and asset information – all from just a phone number or name. Risk assessment and due diligence at your fingertips™.

REALTOR® safety is of the utmost importance. Use the classes and resources below to help develop personal safety strategies.

While being friendly and personable is a key to success for REALTORS®, it is critical to show caution when meeting clients for the first time.

Visit the NAR Safety Resources below for a full list of articles and videos on REALTOR® safety.
