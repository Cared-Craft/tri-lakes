# Meet GSBOR - Greater Springfield Board of Realtors

The Greater Springfield Board of REALTORS® assembled on June 12, 1933 as the leading voice in real estate in Greene, Christian and Webster counties.

GSBOR looks to provide the nebulous, often intangible, yet invaluable commodity of service to REALTORS® in the Springfield area.

All members of the Greater Springfield Board of REALTORS® are pledged to the high standards of our REALTOR® Code of Ethics. The Board, as the vehicle for self-enforcement, promotes ethical conduct among its members and the public — striving always for higher standards for the profession.

## The Vision

To empower our members to excel in professionalism and ethical standards in real estate.

## The Mission

To be the trusted real estate voice advocating for the benefit of our members and our communities.
