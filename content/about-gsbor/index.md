# About GSBOR

The Greater Syracuse Board of REALTORSÂ® (GSBOR) is a professional association dedicated to serving real estate professionals and promoting excellence in the industry throughout Central New York.

## Our Mission

GSBOR is committed to providing our members with the tools, resources, and advocacy they need to succeed in today's dynamic real estate market. We foster professional development, ethical standards, and community engagement among our membership.

## What We Offer

- **Professional Development**: Continuing education courses, workshops, and training programs
- **Advocacy**: Legislative representation and political advocacy for real estate professionals
- **Networking**: Opportunities to connect with fellow professionals and industry leaders
- **Technology**: Access to cutting-edge tools and platforms like MLS NXT
- **Support**: Resources and guidance for both new and experienced professionals

## Our Commitment

We are dedicated to:

- Upholding the highest ethical standards in real estate
- Supporting our members' professional growth and success
- Advocating for property rights and homeownership
- Serving our community through charitable initiatives and volunteer work
- Promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in the real estate industry
