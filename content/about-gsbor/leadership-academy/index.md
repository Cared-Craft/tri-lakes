# Leadership Academy

Leadership Academy teaches GSBOR members new skills they can use in their business and in their personal lives. It gives them a taste of leadership at the local association level and insight into possible involvement at the State and National Association levels. It is designed with the intention to cultivate future leaders for committees and, eventually, the Board of Directors.

GSBOR Leadership Academy was launched in 2018, modeled after Leadership Academies at Missouri REALTORS速 and the National Association of REALTORS速. Those long-running programs have yielded impressive results, as witnessed by Missouri REALTORS速 data.

## Program Structure

The Leadership Academy is designed as a comprehensive program that includes:

- **Monthly Sessions**: Interactive workshops and training sessions
- **Mentorship Opportunities**: Pairing with current board members and leaders
- **Hands-on Experience**: Participation in committee work and association activities
- **Networking Events**: Building relationships with fellow academy members and industry leaders
- **Graduation Recognition**: Formal recognition upon program completion
