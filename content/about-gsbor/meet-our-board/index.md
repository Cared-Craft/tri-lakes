# Meet Our Boards

Our GSBOR and Springfield MLS boards work hard to empower members to excel in professionalism and ethical standards in real estate.

They are the trusted real estate voice advocating for the benefit of our members and communities.

## GSBOR Board of Directors

The Board of Directors of GSBOR is charged with conducting the affairs and establishing all policies of the Greater Springfield Board of REALTORS®. Additionally, our Board of Directors approves the annual budget for the Association and strives to accomplish the vision and mission of GSBOR.

## Springfield MLS Board of Directors

The Board of Directors of the Multilist Service of Springfield REALTORS® works closely with that of the Southern Missouri Regional MLS to establish policies that protect the integrity of data for its member participants/subscribers. Additionally, our Board of Directors approves the annual budget for the operation of the MLS.
