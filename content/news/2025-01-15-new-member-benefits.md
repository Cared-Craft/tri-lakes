# New Member Benefits Program Launched

The Greater Springfield Board of REALTORS® is proud to announce the launch of our enhanced member benefits program, designed to provide even greater value to our members through exclusive discounts, services, and professional development opportunities.

## Enhanced Benefits Package

Our new benefits program includes:

### Professional Services

- **Legal Services**: Discounted rates with approved real estate attorneys
- **Insurance Programs**: Competitive rates on E&O, health, and life insurance
- **Marketing Tools**: Access to premium marketing platforms and materials

### Business Development

- **Continuing Education**: Reduced rates on CE courses and certifications
- **Technology Solutions**: Exclusive pricing on CRM and transaction management tools
- **Networking Events**: Priority access to industry events and conferences

### Personal Benefits

- **Health & Wellness**: Gym memberships and wellness program discounts
- **Travel & Entertainment**: Savings on hotels, car rentals, and dining
- **Financial Services**: Preferred rates on banking and investment services

## How to Access Your Benefits

All current GSBOR members can access these benefits through our member portal. Simply log in with your member credentials and navigate to the "Benefits" section to explore available offers.

New members will receive a welcome packet with detailed information about all available benefits and how to activate them.

## Member Feedback

"The new benefits program demonstrates GSBOR's commitment to supporting our success beyond just MLS access," said Jane Smith, a longtime GSBOR member. "The legal services discount alone will save me hundreds of dollars this year."

## Getting Started

To learn more about the new member benefits program or to activate your benefits, contact our member services team at (417) 883-1655 or visit our member portal.

For non-members interested in joining GSBOR to access these exclusive benefits, visit our [membership page](/new-members/become-a-member) or contact us today.
